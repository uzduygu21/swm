What Is the Path Forward for High-Net-Worth and Ultra-High-Net-Worth Estates?

Reductions to the current estate tax exemptions will undoubtedly have an impact on the tax liabilities for future inheritors. Fortunately, there are solutions and strategies you can implement before the expiration of current estate tax exemption limits. 

Below are six estate planning options available to many high-net-worth and ultra-high-net-worth families that may help to mitigate estate taxes:

  1. Lock in the current gift tax exemption
  2. Structure family businesses strategically
  3. Establish an Irrevocable Life Insurance Trust 
  4. Establish a Qualified Personal Residence Trust
  5. Establish a Grantor Retained Annuity Trust
  6. Accelerate your gift to a 529 account
